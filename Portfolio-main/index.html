Frequently Asked Questions

Find solutions to common questions about web development, design services, project timelines, and how we can address your specific needs

What is your typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary based on complexity, but most standard websites take 2-4 weeks from initial concept to launch. More complex applications or e-commerce sites may require 4-8 weeks. I provide a detailed project timeline during our initial consultation based on your specific requirements and goals.

Do you provide website maintenance and support?

Yes, I offer comprehensive maintenance and support packages to ensure your website remains secure, up-to-date, and performing optimally. This includes regular updates, security monitoring, backup services, and technical support. I can customize a maintenance plan based on your specific needs and budget.

What is your approach to responsive web design?

I follow a mobile-first approach to responsive design, ensuring your website looks and functions perfectly on all devices. This involves using flexible grid layouts, responsive images, and CSS media queries to create a seamless user experience across desktops, tablets, and smartphones. I also test thoroughly on multiple devices and browsers to guarantee consistent performance.

How do we get started with a project?

Getting started is simple! We begin with a free consultation to discuss your project requirements, goals, and timeline. After understanding your needs, I'll provide a detailed proposal with scope, timeline, and pricing. Once approved, we'll sign an agreement and I'll begin the discovery phase to gather all necessary assets and information before starting development.

What happens if I need changes after the project is completed?

I offer ongoing support and maintenance packages for post-launch changes and updates. For minor tweaks, I typically handle them quickly. For larger changes or new features, we can discuss a separate project or add them to a maintenance plan. I aim to be flexible and ensure your website continues to meet your evolving business needs.

What types of businesses have you worked with?

I've worked with a diverse range of clients including startups, small businesses, educational institutions, and e-commerce stores. Each project is unique, but I bring the same level of dedication and expertise regardless of industry or company size. I adapt my approach to meet the specific needs and goals of each client.
